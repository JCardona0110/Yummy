Thoughtful and high quality ingredients lead to better lives and more delicious meals. Our New Asian Cuisine is predominantly Chinese fare but it follows the French tradition.

  • Thoughful ingredients meld Asian flavours with French techniques.
  • Top quality service ensuring the best experiences possible.
  • Unique dishes featured weekly by a number of DaiLo's incredible back of house team.

At the heart of the menu is a love and respect for the dishes Chef Nick Liu ate growing up as the son of Hakka parents in Canada, but importantly, they are the dishes his ancestors have been eating for hundreds of years. Ancient favourites are augmented by Liu's signature flair and updated to suit the western palate and the modern demand for local and sustainable ingredients.

Why Visit DaiLo?

DaiLo is an experience not easily replicated. At DaiLo we take the utmost care to curate your dinner with our awe inspiring Chef's Choice in which our incredible servers and Chef's create a 5 course dinner featuring our favourite dishes along with a number of spectacular features.

Curated dinners for all

Our Chef's Choice tasting menu is made custom for every table. Allergies and restrictions are taken into account and we ensure everyone is able to eat a full and extraordinary dinner.

Incredible features highlighting our young cooks

New features are available weekly from our young cooks along with bangers from our senior Chefs.

Come be taken care of

Our front of house staff of expert servers and sommeliers will ensure every need is taken care of while being encouraged to let their personalities shine to make everyones experience a memorable one.
